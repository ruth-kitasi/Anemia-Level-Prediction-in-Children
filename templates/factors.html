Socioeconomic Factors

Socioeconomic status plays a significant role in the prevalence of anemia. Poverty, lack of access to healthcare, and poor nutrition are major contributing factors. In many low- and middle-income countries, limited resources and access to nutritious food can lead to iron deficiency and other nutritional deficits that cause anemia.
